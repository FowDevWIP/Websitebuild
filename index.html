Who am I?

My names is James, I live in the in Sheffield/UK, a city in the north of England best known for its production of Steel but hopefully soon to be production of the best electronic music in the country! I am 40 years old and recently embarked on a journey to career switch from finance recruitment to software development. This has been driven largely due to wanting to spend my time building something rather than provide service. Conscious that I am perhaps 20 years behind where I could be, I hope AI provides me with an amazing opportunity to learn to code quickly and translate my ideas. I want to use this site as a way to document my journey aswell as showcase my output as an electronic music artist aswell!
